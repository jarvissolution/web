Dark Wallpaper

Dark wallpaper app with best and unique designs with unique black and dark wallpapers

Go to Playstore

Project Overview

In this project we used best wallpapers and best designs while making this app. there are lots of wallpapers available for direct apply. our main focus is to decorate your mobile screens with new and unique wallpapers. Amoled display devices are main focus of this black and dark design wallpapers. in now a days there are most of devices are come with amoled, super amoled display which consumes less battery. for this reason we developed this app.

This project has best user experience like easy to apply wallpaper on main screen and also there are three options for set in specific screen like lock or home or both.

Our this app is available on google play store, India's own Indus App store and we are working to launch on many more..
